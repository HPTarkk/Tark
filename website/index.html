FOUR WAYS TO CONNECT

Hook up however you like.

Personal Hotspot

One phone hosts, everyone else scans its code in the app — no settings, no password. Then just talk, hands-free.

Bluetooth Link

No signal? Link two phones straight over Bluetooth. Your friend shows up by name, connects on its own, and reconnects next time you open the app.

Android & iPhone

Different phones? One Android shares the connection, the iPhone joins with a quick scan. Clear voice the whole way.

Join via Browser

No app? Friends jump in from Safari or Chrome. Send a link or let them scan your code — even over the internet.

HOW IT WORKS BETTER

Simple to use, clever underneath.

AUDIO QUALITY

Crystal clear & light

The app shrinks your voice right down before sending it, so it still sounds like you but barely touches your battery.

NOISE CANCELLATION

Smart noise cleaner

The cleaner has heard thousands of real sounds, so it knows wind, traffic and engine roar the moment they show up and wipes them out while you talk — your voice comes through as you.

NO DELAY

Real-time voice

The app keeps everything smooth, so voices never turn up late on a long ride. You can tweak it in Settings.
